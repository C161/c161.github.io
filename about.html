About


If I knew who I am, I would tell you... Late 2019 I started learning about digital privacy and security, mostly for myself. During the first lockdown in 2020, I created my first website on Neocities to realize a list that is now mostly implemented in my PSA to-do list. Over the course of the last few years, the site underwent multiple name, design, and content changes growing beyond its previous states.
This site utilizes JavaScript for the light-mode toggle, changing between light / dark icons, and for the sorting inside the Messenger Matrix.
